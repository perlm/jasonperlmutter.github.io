--- layout: archive title: "Biophysics" permalink: /biophysics/ author_profile: true --- I have an academic background in Biology, Engineering, and Physics having participated in research during my BS at Cornell University, PhD at the University of Minnesota, Department of Biomedical Engineering and Post-Doctoral work at Brandeis University, Department of Physics. My interests were primarily in using simulations to understand self-assembly of biological molecules. Here's a fun video from a project simulating the assembly of a viral shell (blue) around the viral genome (red). See the paper published at eLife to learn more.
